  Additional Resources Mentioned in Q&A:

  1. Quarto specific page on brand: https://quarto.org/docs/authoring/brand.html

  2. Typography: https://posit-dev.github.io/brand-yml/brand/typography.html

  3. brand.yml + pkgdown: https://github.com/rstudio/bslib/tree/main/pkgdown

  4. LLM brand.yml prompt: https://posit-dev.github.io/brand-yml/articles/llm-brand-yml-prompt/

  5. Inspiration/gallery: https://posit-dev.github.io/brand-yml/inspiration/


  Why we think this is important:

  Consistent company branding in your reports and apps (with your logo, colors, and fonts) can help make your work look more professional, but are often tricky to get right.


  Common challenges we’ve heard from the community:

  1. Excessive manual effort: Applying colors, fonts, and logos across reports, apps, and dashboards takes time and is prone to errors.

  2. Difficult to update: When brand guidelines change, it’s difficult to update all products consistently.

  3. Team consistency: Ensuring all contributors follow branding guidelines is challenging to manage.
