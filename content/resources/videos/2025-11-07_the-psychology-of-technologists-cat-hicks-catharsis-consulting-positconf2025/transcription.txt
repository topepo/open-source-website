<p><a data-t="2026">So we've observed this connection in the world.</a> <a data-t="2030">I wanted to learn a lot more about what can start to help us when we are stuck in those brains in jars cycles.</a> <a data-t="2038">And one inspiration I took from this was a conversation that I had, a really deep, ongoing conversation with a friend of mine.</a> <a data-t="2046">This is not his real name, but he volunteered his story for this.</a> <a data-t="2050">So we'll call him Sean.</a></p>
