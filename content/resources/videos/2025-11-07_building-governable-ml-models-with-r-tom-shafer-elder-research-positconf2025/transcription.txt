<h3>Documentation and testing</h3>

<p><a data-t="453">So this starts as a foundation, and then we can build on it with things like documentation.</a> <a data-t="457">This is probably the least surprising part of this framework, so I won't spend too much</a> <a data-t="461">time on this, but it's super helpful, because it works at multiple timescales.</a></p>
