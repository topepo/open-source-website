<p><a data-t="577">So in terms of temperature, very high values can stress the aquatic life, very low values can slow the metabolism of the organisms living there.</a> <a data-t="654">The specific conductance is typically correlated with clean or polluted water.</a> <a data-t="658">Low values would indicate that typically the water is clean.</a> <a data-t="664">High values could indicate some pollution from industry and so on.</a> <a data-t="670">Dissolved oxygen is one of the main ways also to quantify pollution because if you have very low dissolved oxygen, it usually indicates that zones and fish kills, whereas high dissolved oxygen usually indicates a healthy ecosystem.</a> <a data-t="707">And finally the pH can indicate possible contamination or mineral imbalance in the water, which can cause some stress to the species and so on.</a></p>
