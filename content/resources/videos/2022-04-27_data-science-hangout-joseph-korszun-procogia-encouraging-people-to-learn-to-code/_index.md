(15:27)\n\n⬢ At every point in your career, you’ve written a formula in Excel so just lending that to translate into you know more usable scripts is helpful.\n\n⬢ It starts with identifying what their determined career path is from their own perspective. Some people want to be more statistics oriented, or more ML engineering oriented. You have to understand what their focal point is.\n\n⬢ Help identify someone’s pain point that they have right now and with that - what’s the quickest way to up-skill them in that?\n\n⬢ Have people do some hands-on labs. This can be more beneficial than watching videos or reading textbooks and allows you to get into the code. You’re going to have errors along the way but that’s ok!\n\n⬢ Let new coders know that it’s okay to google and copy/paste code. This can unblock new coders, so that you don’t have to code everything from scratch.
