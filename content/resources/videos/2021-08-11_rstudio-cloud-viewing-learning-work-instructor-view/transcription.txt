<h3>Accessing learner work</h3>

<p><a data-t="27">There are two ways to access learner work.</a> <a data-t="29">One is to click on an individual's name from the Members tab.</a> <a data-t="33">When we do this, we're taken to our learner's projects.</a> <a data-t="36">In this case, our learner has only worked on assignment 1, but we can click into their</a> <a data-t="41">work and see what they've created, as well as run their code.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="45">It's important to note that when we open a learner's project in RStudio Cloud, we are</a> <a data-t="49">not creating a separate copy of their work.</a> <a data-t="52">We're directly accessing their work.</a> <a data-t="55">Any changes we make here will be incorporated into the learner's project.</a></p>
