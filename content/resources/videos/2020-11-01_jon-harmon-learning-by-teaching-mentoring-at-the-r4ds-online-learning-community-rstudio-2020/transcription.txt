<p><a data-t="61">You can find us at r4ds.online, click Slack.</a> <a data-t="65">We have over 4,000 members, about 250 of which are active every week, and we're most famous</a> <a data-t="72">for the Tidy Tuesday project on Twitter, where every Monday we release a data set, and then</a> <a data-t="78">people create visualizations and post their visualizations and their code on Twitter.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="86">We also do the RFeedback Friday hashtag and the Function Friday hashtag, just as a way</a> <a data-t="92">to engage with the RStats community on Twitter.</a></p>
