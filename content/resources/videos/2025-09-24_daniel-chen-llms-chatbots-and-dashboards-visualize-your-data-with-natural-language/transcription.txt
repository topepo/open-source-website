<h3>How LLM conversations work</h3>

<p><a data-t="358">So before I go on, let's talk about what actually</a> <a data-t="361">happens when you have a conversation with a chatbot.</a> <a data-t="363">And then I'll show you a couple of tools</a> <a data-t="366">on how we can leverage having that conversation.</a> <a data-t="368">And those of you who were in the workshop yesterday,</a> <a data-t="370">this is literally like the slides from the workshop</a> <a data-t="371">yesterday.</a></p>
