<h3>Using a sharing link to invite learners</h3>

<p><a data-t="29">To add multiple individuals to our workspace, let's click on the Members button at the</a> <a data-t="34">top of our workspace, and then click on Options to open the Access menu.</a> <a data-t="39">From here, let's invite multiple individuals using the sharing link.</a> <a data-t="43">This method will give us a link that we can distribute via email or on a course webpage,</a> <a data-t="48">allowing all of our learners to access the workspace on RStudio Cloud.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="53">For the initial role, we'll choose Contributor, and then take a look at the permissions at</a> <a data-t="57">the bottom of the page.</a> <a data-t="59">We can choose what contributors are allowed to do within the workspace by selecting or</a> <a data-t="63">deselecting the options presented.</a> <a data-t="66">Once we've made our selections, we can click on Copy Sharing Link and distribute it as</a> <a data-t="70">needed.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="73">Once all of our learners have joined the shared workspace, we can disable the existing link</a> <a data-t="77">by resetting the sharing link or by setting our workspace to Invitation Required, which</a> <a data-t="82">will mean new learners to the workspace will have to be added via the Add Member button</a> <a data-t="87">from within the Members section of our workspace.</a></p>
