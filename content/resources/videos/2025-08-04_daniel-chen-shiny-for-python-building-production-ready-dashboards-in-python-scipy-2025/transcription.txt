<p><a data-t="4674">And I wanted to talk about the core thing that makes Shiny special, which is reactivity.</a> <a data-t="4680">There's a couple of links to other presentations that have also talked about reactivity.</a> <a data-t="4682">Shiny for Python has been around for a while.</a> <a data-t="4686">So yes, all of those talks are in 2023.</a> <a data-t="4691">They're all still very, very relevant to this particular topic on reactivity.</a></p>
