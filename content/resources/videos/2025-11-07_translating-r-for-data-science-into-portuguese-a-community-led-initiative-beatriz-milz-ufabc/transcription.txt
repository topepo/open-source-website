<h3>How we got people to contribute</h3>

<p><a data-t="553">So how we get so many people to contribute in this project. So there are three main things that I thought about when thinking about this</a> <a data-t="559">talk. <p><a data-t="634">And the second thing that was important for us in this project was to show the path. As I said before, the book is stored in a GitHub</a> <a data-t="643">repository.
