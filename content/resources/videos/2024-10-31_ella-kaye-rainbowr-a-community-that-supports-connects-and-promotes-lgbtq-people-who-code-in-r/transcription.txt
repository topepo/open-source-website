<h3>How rainbowR began</h3>

<p><a data-t="15">At the useR conference in 2017</a> <a data-t="17">I was chatting with the guy who I just met and I mentioned in passing my wife.</a> <a data-t="24">I didn't know it at the time</a> <a data-t="25">But the guy I was speaking to is also gay and it turns out that he was finding</a> <a data-t="30">Socializing at the conference at such a large conference to be rather overwhelming</a> <a data-t="34">But as soon as he knew he was speaking to someone else who's also gay, he felt a sense of camaraderie that he could relax</a></p>

<p><a data-t="41">from there we got to talking about the possibility for, the need for even, an</a> <a data-t="47">LGBTQ plus community within the broader R community and that's how rainbowR was born</a> <a data-t="54">That was seven years ago. The community is now going to over a hundred people</a></p>

<p><a data-t="59">From our time together today. I'd like you to take away that and why we exist as a community</a> <a data-t="65">Some of what we do and how you can get involved</a></p>

<h3>What rainbowR does</h3>

<p><a data-t="69">we exist to connect support and promote</a> <a data-t="73">LGBTQ plus people in the R community and to spread awareness of LGBTQ plus issues through data-driven activism</a> <a data-t="81">When it comes to connecting, supporting and promoting people we have two main initiatives</a></p>

<p><a data-t="86">We run monthly meetups over zoom usually on the fourth Wednesday of the month</a> <a data-t="91">These are really relaxed, friendly, supportive sessions where people come together to chat mostly about R</a> <a data-t="99">It's an opportunity to ask and answer questions</a> <a data-t="102">To demonstrate packages or resources you've come across recently and find helpful</a> <a data-t="106">Or to show and tell your own work in a really supportive setting</a></p>

<p><a data-t="112">We run a buddy scheme to encourage deeper connections within the community</a> <a data-t="116">Which gives people an opportunity to be randomly paired with a buddy in the community</a> <a data-t="122">We open up every three months a form</a> <a data-t="124">Where if you want a buddy you can fill it in with a little bit of information about yourself and your interests</a> <a data-t="130">We then run an R script which gathers the form responses</a> <a data-t="134">Randomly allocates buddy pairs and then automatically generates and sends emails to each pair</a> <a data-t="140">Where the email is pre-populated with information from the form</a> <a data-t="146">And suggestions of things that you might want to talk about or do together</a></p>

<p><a data-t="151">We do this because we know it can be nerve-wracking to meet somebody new</a> <a data-t="156">And we want to help get those conversations started</a> <a data-t="159">The next round of the buddy scheme will allocate pairs at the beginning of September</a> <a data-t="164">The sign-up sheet is already open. You can see the website for more details. I'll share the link again at the end</a></p>

<h3>Data-driven activism</h3>

<p><a data-t="170">On to data-driven activism</a> <a data-t="173">We host a repo, Tidy Rainbow</a> <a data-t="176">Which contains data sets with data relevant to LGBTQ plus issues</a> <a data-t="182">The data is there for anybody to use and can be used in a variety of ways</a> <a data-t="186">For example, it can be used to practice data visualization or data analysis along the lines of the Tidy Tuesday project</a> <a data-t="194">Or it can be used to provide example data sets when teaching or writing blog posts</a> <a data-t="201">Something that hopefully will be of interest to allies as well</a></p>

<p><a data-t="205">We're about to start our first ever book club, the book under discussion</a> <a data-t="208">Is going to be Queer Data, using gender, sex and sexuality data for action by Kevin Guillen</a> <a data-t="214">This book club is open for everyone, LGBTQ plus folks and allies alike</a> <a data-t="220">The book club is going to run in the autumn. There are more details on our website</a></p>
