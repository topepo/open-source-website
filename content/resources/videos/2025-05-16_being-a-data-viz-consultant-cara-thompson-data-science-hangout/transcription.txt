<h3>Common pitfalls in data viz</h3>

<p><a data-t="2647">What are some common pitfalls in DataVis and how can you avoid them?</a></p>

<p><a data-t="2667">Yeah, I mean, there are pitfalls. <p><a data-t="2716">Often as data people, we come at things with like, oh, look at all this data, look at all these patterns, how can I show them all?</a> <a data-t="2722">When in actual fact, the question is, what do my users need?</a> <a data-t="2722">What is the first thing that should stand out from the visualization and what could I put behind the curtain, either with some interactivity or with extra graphs in it?</a> <a data-t="2733">I think that to me is a big pitfall that I see a lot.</a> <a data-t="2733">It's that desire to show everything that you've done in a graph, which isn't super helpful for the people.</a></p>
