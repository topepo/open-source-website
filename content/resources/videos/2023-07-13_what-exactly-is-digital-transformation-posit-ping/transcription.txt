<p><a data-t="4">Digital transformation is a tough thing to explain.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="8">The way I try to explain digital transformation to anyone is,</a> <a data-t="11">imagine a river and you get to stand beside that river as an advisor to a company.</a> <a data-t="16">And constantly floating down that river are little crates and in each one of those crates</a> <a data-t="21">is a new technology or a fad or a new product.</a> <a data-t="28">And it's your job as a company to quickly</a> <a data-t="28">evaluate each crate going down the river and identify, does that have something in it that</a> <a data-t="32">can help me?</a> <a data-t="35">Does that have something in it that could hurt me?</a> <a data-t="37">And how hard is it going to be for</a> <a data-t="37">me to jump in the river and fish out the crate and drag it back and try to make use out of it?</a></p>

<p><a data-t="42">And that's the really hard part.</a> <a data-t="46">And a company that tries to reach every single one in the</a> <a data-t="46">river and pull it out is going to quickly find themselves exhausted and overwhelmed</a> <a data-t="50">and have a hard time trying to cobble together what they just pulled out of the river.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="54">A company that's falling behind says that's energy and effort that's not necessarily always</a> <a data-t="58">worth it.</a> <a data-t="62">So I'm going to just stick with what I've got.</a> <a data-t="62">I'm going to let those sail on,</a> <a data-t="62">pass me to the next person standing behind me on the side of the river.</a></p>

<h3>What good digital transformation looks like</h3>

<p><a data-t="66">But a company that's really good at digital transformation, at applying technology for</a> <a data-t="70">competitive advantage, is good at seeing that crate float by, quickly deciding, is that crate</a> <a data-t="76">worth it?</a> <a data-t="80">And then once I've got it on the shore, can I quickly put it to work so I can get back to</a> <a data-t="80">looking at the river and seeing what's coming down?</a> <a data-t="85">As a company, if you have a center of</a> <a data-t="85">excellence of data scientists who are developing things, really valuable things, but they're hard</a> <a data-t="90">for you to leverage in other departments, other groups, or even commercially publicly,</a> <a data-t="95">Posit Connect in this context was one of those that you quickly see coming down and say,</a> <a data-t="100">like, okay, this is going to work.</a> <a data-t="104">To me, it's a little underdog technology that helps us</a> <a data-t="104">bridge a really big gap.</a></p>
