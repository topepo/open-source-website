If you want funding you're a Data Scientist. If you HAVE funding it doesn't matter.\n\nFrank: No right answer (DS vs. DA)… only different ways to think about it at different times in different contexts.\n\nJordan: I've usually been working in a \"\"Science\"\" (science/research) domain at companies.  To this end, I've sort of been the \"\"Data\"\" Scientist, maybe not \"\"Data Scientist\"\"\n\n*What about business intelligence?*\n\nAsmae: I use data analyst and data scientist interchangeably.
