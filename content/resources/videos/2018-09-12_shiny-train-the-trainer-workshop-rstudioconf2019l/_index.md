That's a great question, I'm glad you asked. \n\nRegister at https://rstd.io/conf\nLearn more at https://rstd.io/conf-agenda\n\n--------------------\n\nShiny Train-the-Trainer Certification Workshop - 2 Day\n\n- Day 1 of the course will be co-taught by Mine Cetinkaya-Rundel and Garrett Grolemund, RStudio Data Scientists and Professional Educators. \n- On Day 2, Mine will teach the Shiny track and Garrett will teach the Tidyverse track.\n\nThis two-day workshop will equip you to teach R effectively. We will draw on RStudio's experience teaching R to recommend tips for designing, teaching, and supporting short R courses.\n\nOn Day 1 of the course, you will learn practical activities that you can use immediately to improve your presentation style, learning outcomes, and student engagement. You will leave the class with a cognitive model of learning that you can use to develop your own effective workshops or courses within your organization. The course materials will build on RStudio's Mastering Shiny workshop as well as the upcoming book from the author of the Shiny package, Joe Cheng, and they will cover the entire lifecycle of a Shiny app: build ➡️ improving ➡️ share. Participants will receive the course materials for teaching Mastering Shiny. You should take this workshop if you work as a training partner and want to qualify as an RStudio Certified Shiny Instructor or if you are an advocate for R in your organization. You should be proficient in Shiny already and be prepared to submit examples of your work. Prior teaching experience is helpful, but not required. Please bring a laptop and a device that has video recording capabilities (such as a laptop or cell phone). \n\nInstructors: Garrett Grolemund,  Mine Çetinkaya-Rundel"
