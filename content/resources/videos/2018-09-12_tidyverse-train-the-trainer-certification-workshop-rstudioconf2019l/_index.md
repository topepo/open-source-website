That's a great question, I'm glad you asked. \n\nRegister at https://rstd.io/conf\nLearn more at https://rstd.io/conf-agenda\n \n\n------------------------\n\nTidyverse Train-the-Trainer Certification Workshop - 2 Days\n\n- Day 1 of the course will be co-taught by Mine Cetinkaya-Rundel and Garrett Grolemund, RStudio Data Scientists and Professional Educators. \n- On Day 2, Mine will teach the Shiny track and Garrett will teach the Tidyverse track.\n\nThis two-day workshop will equip you to teach R effectively. We will draw on RStudio's experience teaching R to recommend tips for designing, teaching, and supporting short R courses.\n\nOn Day 1 of the course, you will learn practical activities that you can use immediately to improve your presentation style, learning outcomes, and student engagement. We will use Master the Tidyverse, which is an award-winning two-day workshop developed by RStudio, as an example. Participants will receive the course materials for teaching Master the Tidyverse. You should take this workshop if you work for a training partner and want to qualify as an RStudio Certified Tidyverse Instructor or if you are an advocate for R in your organization. You should be proficient in the Tidyverse already and be prepared to submit examples of your work. Prior teaching experience is helpful, but not required.
