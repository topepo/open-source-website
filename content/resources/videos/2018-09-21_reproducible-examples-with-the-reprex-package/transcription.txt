<p><a data-t="663">Okay, so we're going to get back to the package and to reproducible examples now.</a> <a data-t="667">So I wanted to give a brief intro, like what on earth in my life drove me to make this one of my missions.</a> <a data-t="676">Before I joined RStudio, I was a professor at the University of British Columbia, and I had a course called Stat 545 that has a lot of content online to this day.</a> <a data-t="688">The course continues, so you could go there.</a> <a data-t="691">And I ran this course entirely on GitHub.</a> <a data-t="694">And so it meant that all of my dialogue with students, both sort of me to the whole class and me talking to individual students,</a> <a data-t="706">took place in GitHub issues.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="708">And I actually analyzed my GitHub usage in the course, and I found that every fall I was participating in at least 300, maybe 500 GitHub issue threads.</a> <a data-t="721">And that's just in my teaching life.</a> <a data-t="724">So I spend a great deal of time talking about R in those places and solving people's R problems.</a> <a data-t="731">And to do that well, I actually wanted to use executable R code, and the friction involved in making that look good started to drive me crazy.</a></p>
