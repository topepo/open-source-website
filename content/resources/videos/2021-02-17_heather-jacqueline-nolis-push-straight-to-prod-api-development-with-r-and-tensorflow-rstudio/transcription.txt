<h3>Lessons learned</h3>

<p><a data-t="766">But we learned a lot of stuff in doing this, and so I think that the first thing that we</a> <a data-t="771">learned is, you know, people came to us in the beginning and said you have to write this</a> <a data-t="774">in Python, we won't support you doing it in R, but we tested our APIs at 50 times T</a> <a data-t="780">mobile's maximum load and R performed just fine, so for us, using R and plumber was at</a> <a data-t="785">very close parity performance wise with Python and Flask, and then R was really good for</a> <a data-t="789">us to do the quick data exploration that got us the idea to build this model in the first</a> <a data-t="794">place, and then the shiny demos are what really, really sold our project, and there's not really</a> <a data-t="799">an equivalent for Python, you can't just quickly throw a few lines of code and take it to a</a> <a data-t="803">business person and have it passed around like that, and most importantly, language</a> <a data-t="808">is never the fail point for our project.</a></p>
