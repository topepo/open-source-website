<p><a data-t="2871">The next question is, why did we make Quarto, and how is it different from R Markdown?</a> <a data-t="2879">Really quick on why we made it, the R Markdown ecosystem had a bunch of really awesome features</a> <a data-t="2886">that people really love, and as JJ mentioned in a recent presentation, it has a requirement for</a> <a data-t="2893">the knitr package.
