<p><a data-t="0">With your </a><a href="/software/rstudio/">RStudio</a><a data-t="0"> Cloud account, you'll have access to your own personal workspace,</a> <a data-t="8">as well as the ability to create additional workspaces.</a> <a data-t="12">As an educator, you might find it helpful to create a single workspace for each of your</a> <a data-t="16">classes or workshops.</a></p>

<h3>Creating a new workspace</h3>

<p><a data-t="34">Once we log in to RStudio Cloud, navigate to the menu on the left by clicking here.</a> <a data-t="39">We can also choose to keep this menu open by clicking on this pin.</a> <a data-t="44">Here we see my personal workspace, as well as a series of workspaces that I've created</a> <a data-t="48">or joined.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="49">We can create a new workspace by clicking on this New Space button and naming our new</a> <a data-t="53">workspace.</a> <a data-t="55">For the purposes of this example, I'll call our workspace Fall 2021 Data Visualization.</a></p>
