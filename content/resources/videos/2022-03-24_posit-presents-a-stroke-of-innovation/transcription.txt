<h3>The model and the tech stack</h3>

<p><a data-t="450">Our model is a very simple one.</a> <a data-t="454">It's a linear spline model.</a> <a data-t="456">And the only inputs are...</a> <a data-t="458">Time of the day.</a> <a data-t="459">Day of the week.</a> <a data-t="460">And time of the year.</a> <a data-t="461">Every five minutes we have a process in WebMethods that reads data from the swimming pool gates.</a> <a data-t="469">And stores them in our data lake.</a> <a data-t="472">We then use the pins package.</a> <a data-t="475">We get pinned linear models from our </a><a href="/software/rstudio/">RStudio</a><a data-t="475"> Connect.</a> <a data-t="480">And we then predict the duration.</a> <a data-t="484">Then we pin that results table in our RStudio Connect.</a> <a data-t="488">And that's what our </a><a href="/software/shiny-r/">Shiny</a><a data-t="488"> app accesses.</a></p>
