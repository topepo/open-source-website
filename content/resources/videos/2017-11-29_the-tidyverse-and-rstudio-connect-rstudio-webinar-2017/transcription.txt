<h3>Content creators and consumers</h3>

<p><a data-t="169">Communicating involves two parties.</a> <a data-t="171">The first party is the content creator.</a> <a data-t="173">Who is the content creator?</a> <a data-t="174">That is the data scientists, the domain experts, or the app developers.</a> <a data-t="179">Their job is to understand, measure, predict, and optimize systems and analyses on top of</a> <a data-t="186">their data.</a> <a data-t="188">The output of that understanding is typically artifacts, documents, applications, or plots</a> <a data-t="194">or whatnot.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="195">Then you have the content consumers.</a> <a data-t="199">These are the people that are the managers, the peers, maybe other students.</a> <a data-t="203">These people may or may not understand R or have a great background in analytics.</a> <a data-t="209">Their job is to take what you learn, what you do, and make decisions and take actions</a> <a data-t="216">and see results.</a> <a data-t="218">These content consumers are extremely important in taking advantage of the artifacts that</a> <a data-t="223">are being created.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="224">A typical process workflow, and this is what I am very familiar with, I am an analyst by</a> <a data-t="230">training.</a> <a data-t="231">I have used R for a long time.</a> <a data-t="232">I go into R and create my analysis.</a> <a data-t="237">When I am done, I make a report and I send it on to the consumers by email or SharePoint</a> <a data-t="242">or put it on the file share somewhere.</a> <a data-t="246">That is a very traditional way of sharing your analysis.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="249">What happens when you do that, invariably, somebody asks a question.</a> <a data-t="253">They say, there is an error here, or I want to see more, or I want to go deeper.</a> <a data-t="259">The content creator goes back to work, opens up their code, and creates a new artifact.</a> <a data-t="266">That artifact is then pushed back to the content consumers and the cycle repeats.</a> <a data-t="271">This can be a time-consuming cycle.</a> <a data-t="273">The question is, can we do better, can we be more efficient in the way that this cycle</a> <a data-t="278">is managed?</a></p>

<h3>The case for self-service analytics</h3>
