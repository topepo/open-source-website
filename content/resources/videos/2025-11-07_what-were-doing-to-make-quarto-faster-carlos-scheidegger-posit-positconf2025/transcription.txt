<h3>Rewriting the front end in Rust</h3>

<p><a data-t="187">And the answer is like, what if we sort of took over that little bit part of Pandoc ourselves, we know what the markdown looks like, we can try to sort of take over it.</a> <a data-t="195">And so this is really a story about what we've been working on for the last six months, me being me.</a> <a data-t="202">We are sort of taking over the front end for Pandoc and sort of parsing this ourselves.</a> <a data-t="206">If you heard </a><a href="/software/air/">AIR</a><a data-t="206">, if you heard the story about Ruff, if you heard all of this, this is the same story.</a> <a data-t="210">We're just rewriting the entire front end in Rust.</a></p>
