<h3>Positron Assistant</h3>

<p><a data-t="613">That's Positron, but today's focus is really about highlighting some of the really cool AI LLM integrations with Positron.</a> <a data-t="617">The first one that we're going to introduce you to is something called Positron Assistant.</a> <a data-t="622">This is an AI client that's actually built directly into Positron.</a> <a data-t="623">It's nothing you have to add additionally.</a> <a data-t="624">It's right there.</a> <a data-t="626">You can start using it.</a></p>
