<h3>The RStudio virtuous cycle</h3>

<p><a data-t="1609">All right, so just to recap, our studio's goal is to create an organization that can</a> <a data-t="1613">have a durable contribution in this world by creating very high-quality open-source</a> <a data-t="1619">software that is accessible to anyone, anywhere, regardless of their economic means.</a> <a data-t="1622">In order to make sure that the business would be sustainable longer-term, we decided to</a> <a data-t="1626">build a software-only business that sells commercial versions of our open-source server</a> <a data-t="1630">products.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="1635">These products help enterprises adopt R and open science tools in production environments.</a> <a data-t="1639">The differentiation between our open-source products and our commercial products lie in</a> <a data-t="1643">capabilities around security, authentication, monitoring, tuning, scaling, metrics, collaboration,</a> <a data-t="1648">sharing.</a> <a data-t="1649">You get a commercial license and premium support.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="1659">Our hope is that we can sustain this virtuous cycle, which allows us to then invest in the</a> <a data-t="1663">areas of the greatest needs for the R community.</a> <a data-t="1667">So far, things have been going really well.</a> <a data-t="1671">The open-source packages make R more and more accessible, which in turn drives the adoption</a> <a data-t="1675">of R in enterprises, which hopefully creates more demand for our commercial products, which</a> <a data-t="1679">then allows us to fund even more open-source work.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="1684">Put it another way, our open-source products create value for everyone, and our commercial</a> <a data-t="1689">products help businesses leverage that value.</a> <a data-t="1692">We believe that this gives the open-source community benefits because you have talented,</a> <a data-t="1697">paid open-source developers creating a growing number of free, high-quality R packages that</a> <a data-t="1702">can be consistently maintained.</a></p>

<blockquote><a data-t="1684">Our open-source products create value for everyone, and our commercial</a> <a data-t="1689">products help businesses leverage that value.</a></blockquote>

<p><a data-t="1705">Commercial customers benefit from a financially viable and sustainable ecosystem of these</a> <a data-t="1709">open-source tools and packages, along with products that meet their enterprise IT standards</a> <a data-t="1713">for deployment and production.</a> <a data-t="1716">A thriving ecosystem of education, training, and services can bring energy, new ideas,</a> <a data-t="1721">and talent to create and share data products at a fraction of the cost and hopefully improve</a> <a data-t="1726">our societies and our world.</a></p>
