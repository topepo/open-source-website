<h3>Who is an R champion?</h3>

<p><a data-t="47">So who is an R champion?</a> <a data-t="51">Well, probably a lot of you here are R champions.</a> <a data-t="56">But these are some ideas that I came up with as I was thinking about what an R champion is.</a> <a data-t="62">So an R champion is someone who uses R to accomplish the core goals and objectives of their job, promotes the use of R within their group and broader ecosystem that they live in.</a> <a data-t="109">They contribute to the R community through RStudio community and Stack Overflow and other mediums, advocates for and takes ownership of R tooling in their environment.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="141">And then an R champion is also someone who stays up to date on all things R and freely disseminates this information.</a> <a data-t="147">So within their group at work, but also through other mediums.</a> <a data-t="152">So through community, through R meetups and other channels.</a></p>
