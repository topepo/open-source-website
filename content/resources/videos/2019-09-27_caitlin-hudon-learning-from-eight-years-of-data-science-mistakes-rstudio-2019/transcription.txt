<h3>Mistake zone four: infrastructure and team</h3>

<p><a data-t="888">One last problem that we'll talk about is infrastructure and team.</a> <a data-t="888">So one of the roles that I took on was to work on the algorithm.</a> <a data-t="904">So we had a company that a large portion of their business ran on this single set of machine learning algorithms that were in production.</a> <a data-t="910">The fact that we refer to it as the algorithm should tell you sort of everything you need to know about what the rest of the business understood about how this all worked.</a> <a data-t="919">So we had a couple of data scientists leave at the same time.</a> <a data-t="924">One of them was lucky enough to sit down with us and spend a whole week teaching us how the algorithm worked, the ins and outs, that sort of thing.</a> <a data-t="933">When it came time for us to pick up the algorithm and just continue to work with it, it was super scary.</a> <a data-t="940">We did not have the documentation that we actually needed to be able to understand the algorithm, like inside out.</a> <a data-t="946">So we had to spend a ton of time trying to engineer the code, figure out where these things came from, and then explain it to other people.</a></p>
