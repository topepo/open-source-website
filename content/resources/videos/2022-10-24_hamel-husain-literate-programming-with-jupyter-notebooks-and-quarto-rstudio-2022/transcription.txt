<p><a data-t="66">So in this talk I'm going to be introducing nbdev and nbdev and some associated tools kind of compose like a dialect of Python</a> <a data-t="122">and these tools allow me and other developers to be very productive.</a> <a data-t="126">And this dialect of Python focuses on Python's dynamic properties and really focuses on REPL driven development so that you can build software with kind of this REPL driven development and software engineering in mind.</a></p>
