<h3>The Posit Academy apprenticeship model</h3>

<p><a data-t="32">The attractive thing about this workshop is that it is a Posit Academy course.</a> <a data-t="37">That means you'll learn by doing a six-week online apprenticeship with a Posit mentor</a> <a data-t="42">and several co-apprentices.</a> <a data-t="43">You'll meet as a group twice a week to work through a realistic project and to get feedback</a> <a data-t="49">on your work.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="51">At the first meeting each week, you'll have a chance to just work together and ask questions</a> <a data-t="55">with your mentor and your fellow students.</a> <a data-t="58">At the second meeting, you'll present your work to everyone else.</a> <a data-t="62">This meeting is not optional, but it's the place where you'll get the personalized</a> <a data-t="66">feedback that will help you become a better data scientist.</a></p>
