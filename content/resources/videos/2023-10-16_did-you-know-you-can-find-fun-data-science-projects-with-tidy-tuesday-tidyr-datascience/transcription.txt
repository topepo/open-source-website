<p><a data-t="0">Looking for a fun data science project?</a> <a data-t="7">Tidy Tuesday releases new datasets every week.</a> <a data-t="7">The topics are anything from most haunted places in the USA to information on all of</a> <a data-t="12">Taylor Swift's albums.</a> <a data-t="17">Each dataset comes with an explanation of the data and where</a> <a data-t="17">it comes from, a data dictionary of all the columns, and a script to clean and import</a> <a data-t="24">the data in the R language.</a> <a data-t="31">People share their exploration online with hashtag TidyTuesday.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="32">All data is open source and free to use and share.</a> <a data-t="38">I'm enchanted with this project.</a></p>
