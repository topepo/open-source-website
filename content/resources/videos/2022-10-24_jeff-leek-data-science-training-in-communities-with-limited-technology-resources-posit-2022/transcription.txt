<h3>The future of Datatrail</h3>

<p><a data-t="2036">So our Datatrail model has three big components now.</a> <a data-t="2040">There's colleges and universities or training partners in general.</a> <a data-t="2035">They provide tutors and educational support.</a> <a data-t="2040">Then there's hiring partners or mentors.</a> <a data-t="2040">They provide the mentors and opportunities for the people once they finish the training program.</a> <a data-t="2045">And then we have nonprofit and community-based partners who do the hard work of supporting the whole person.</a> <a data-t="2051">Because when you're doing a training program and you're coming from a community that doesn't have a lot of resources, small things can cause big problems if you don't have the support you need to get through the training program.</a> <a data-t="2062">And so the three groups working together can really provide that infrastructure, that underlying infrastructure that gives somebody the opportunity to come from a community without a lot of opportunity into a place where they can be a leader in the field.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="2096">So this is how our program works now.</a> <a data-t="2102">From January to June, we're recruiting young people.</a> <a data-t="2104">We're getting into schools.</a> <a data-t="2106">We're providing content online.</a> <a data-t="2106">We're trying to get people excited about data science.</a> <a data-t="2110">And then from June to September, we're doing our training program.</a> <a data-t="2113">We're actually running the data trail courses.</a> <a data-t="2115">They're going through, they're getting paid as they complete the courses, and they're finishing the program.</a> <a data-t="2120">And we're also recruiting mentors.</a> <a data-t="2122">We're recruiting the people that are ultimately going to hire and mentor and manage the young people in their first opportunity.</a> <a data-t="2128">Then from September to June, they do an internship.</a> <a data-t="2131">This is supported by Urban Alliance.</a> <a data-t="2133">And this is a 10-hour-a-week internship where they get paid.</a> <a data-t="2137">It's virtual, and they get mentorship a couple of hours a week from a mentor at a hiring company or a partner.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="2145">This is how the program works now.</a> <a data-t="2147">And so there's an opportunity for you to get involved with us at DataTrail if you're interested.</a> <a data-t="2151">There's two different ways.</a> <a data-t="2153">One is the more heavy-duty way, which is start your own data trail in your own community.</a> <a data-t="2158">And the other way is to hire or mentor data trail graduates from our program.</a></p>
