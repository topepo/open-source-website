<h3>How Shiny Assistant works</h3>

<p><a data-t="396">Now, if you look at Shiny Assistant interface here, there's really two important parts. There's the Assistant, which is the chatbot. <p><a data-t="437">It can get you started on a project really quickly. <p><a data-t="463">The other component is Shiny Live.
