<p><a data-t="1024">And deploying just means putting a model in production,</a> <a data-t="1027">which is kind of a loaded sentence because what is production?</a> <a data-t="1031">It looks very different for different people.</a> <a data-t="1035">But in general, we're going to say that deploying a model is anywhere</a> <a data-t="1039">that is not on your local laptop.</a> <a data-t="1041">This could be inside of an application.</a> <a data-t="1044">This can be just kind of living somewhere or anywhere in between.</a></p>
