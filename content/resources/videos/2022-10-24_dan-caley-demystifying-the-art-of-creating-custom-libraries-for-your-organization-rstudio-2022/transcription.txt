<p><a data-t="136">So the things that we're going to cover is, you know, why package your functions in a library?</a> <a data-t="140">Because that's what a library is.</a> <a data-t="141">It's just a bunch of functions.</a> <a data-t="142">You know, and we're just going to briefly talk about functions and how to create a function and then create a library and then document your function and then how to install your library.</a> <a data-t="153">And how to install your library is going to be the important part, because that's going to allow you to share it either via GitHub, through, you know, your GitHub's firewall, or, you know, you can have it saved in a local directory.</a></p>
