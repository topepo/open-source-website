<p><a data-t="179">So we're going to showcase how we utilize our high-tech motion capture system that we call FOCAL and leverage Posit Connect to answer this question.</a> <a data-t="185">So FOCAL is probably the most high-end and in-depth research tool that we have at Ping, and it</a> <a data-t="190">essentially allows us to track the golfer's swing from the time they address the ball</a> <a data-t="194">through that loading phase to just after impact.</a> <a data-t="198">And we're doing this by tracking some small reflective markers that we place along the club head and on the shaft to model the</a> <a data-t="203">club as a body as it moves through space.</a> <a data-t="208">FOCAL is quite a bit different than some of the other performance measurement systems in golf.</a> <a data-t="212">If you've ever watched golf on the weekends, they have the launch monitors that are kind of tracing the ball flight and what the ball is doing.</a> <a data-t="220">But with FOCAL, we can actually see exactly how the golfer manipulated the club and got to impact.</a></p>
