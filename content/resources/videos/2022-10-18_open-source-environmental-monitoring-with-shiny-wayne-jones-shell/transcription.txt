<h3>What GWSDAT is designed to do</h3>

<p><a data-t="410">Before I give a demonstration of the tool in action, I think it's worth pointing out what</a> <a data-t="416">it's actually designed to do. <p><a data-t="459">And to do that, we use smoothing statistics, and I'll give more examples of this during the</a> <a data-t="464">demonstration as well, both smoothing in one-dimensional time series, for example using</a> <a data-t="472">local linear regression. <p><a data-t="535">Worth pointing out that it was with the help of the University of Glasgow,</a> <a data-t="540">the underlying methodology for the spatial temporal statistics is penalised spline,</a> <a data-t="547">line-based methodology, and we went for this way more than anything because of the speed of</a> <a data-t="552">computation.
