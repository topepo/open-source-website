<p><a data-t="192">Unfortunately, students are increasingly resistant to this technique.</a> <a data-t="196">So how can we potentially update our pedagogy to reflect this new reality?</a> <a data-t="200">Well, I don't exactly ask that same question anymore about the religious income data set,</a> <a data-t="205">because I get a lot of AI-generated responses back.</a> <a data-t="208">Some are great.</a> <a data-t="209">Some are awful.</a> <a data-t="211">Some are just plain hilarious.</a></p>
