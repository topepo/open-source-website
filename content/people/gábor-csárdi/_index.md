Gábor Csárdi is a Senior Software Engineer at Posit, based in Barcelona. He is a prolific contributor to the R ecosystem, having worked on over 100 open-source packages including foundational tools like [pak]({{<ref "software/pak" >}}), [cli]({{<ref "software/cli" >}}), [processx]({{<ref "software/processx" >}}), [ps]({{<ref "software/ps" >}}), and [pkgdepends]({{<ref "software/pkgdepends" >}}). His work focuses on package development infrastructure, system interfaces, and developer tools that make R programming more efficient and reliable.
